Warning Signs

Description

Created over the course of a day and a half for my first hackathon at AThack!, an organization working to end human trafficking. Warning Signs is a tool designed to help identify if a person is at risk of being sexually exploited or experiencing domestic abuse through a randomized survey.

Technologies used

  • AngularJS
  • Express.js
  • Node.js
  • MongoDB
  • Mongoose
  • Twitter Bootstrap
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
